## Style Recommendations

> [!TIP]
> **Illustration Style Guidelines**
> - **Style:** Modern flat with subtle gradients, warm and approachable
> - **People:** Diverse, abstract/simplified faces (inclusive representation)
> - **Color palette:** Primary use of Amethyst Smoke & Blackberry Cream with complementary warm neutrals
> - **Mood:** Optimistic, collaborative, empowering (not stressed about money)
> - **Consistency:** Same character style across all people illustrations

### Do's
- ✅ Show couples working together (collaboration is core to Winnie)
- ✅ Use metaphors that make finance approachable (gardens, journeys, building)
- ✅ Include subtle details that show progress and positivity
- ✅ Keep illustrations clean with whitespace

### Don'ts
- ❌ Avoid scary/anxiety-inducing money imagery
- ❌ No overly corporate or clinical financial graphics
- ❌ Avoid gendered stereotypes in couple representations
- ❌ Don't use green as the primary "money" color (use brand purple instead)
